Project Introduction


Q1. (NLP) What are the major topics discussed in Reddit related to Russia Ukraine conflict?

Business goal: The goal is to find out topics related to Russia Ukraine Conflict and to provide guidance to develop idea about the project.


Technical proposal: Use NLP to convert unstructured text data into a numerical format. The resulting matrix will be used to topic model from large collections of documents and provide features to classify documents. Use Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) to model the topic and use spaCy to remove numbers and punctuation and lemmatize the result. Visualize the Topic Modeling using pyLDAvis.

Q2. Is there any significant difference between attitudes of teenagers and adults towards the RU event throughout the whole period?

Business goal: We want to get the attitude difference between teenagers and adults towards different small events in the whole RU conflict, which can be mapped to the time axis. Then we can make different policies accordingly to these two groups.


Technical proposal: We first group the data by date/month and over_18. Then we make sentiment analysis to each reddit comment/submission. We collect statistics of sentiment scores in different groups. Finally we have the two sentiment lines plot and map that to small events.

Q2. Which topics/kinds of submissions/comments are more popular?

Business goal: We want to measure how much submissions/comments focus on certain topics/celebrities/events. By doing that, we can look into about which topics people are concerned for the RU conflict.


Technical proposal: We can simply do the naive word extraction and statistics for each submission/comment. A more convincing way is use the method of word2vec/doc2vec to compute the averaged similarities to each topic. A low dimension projection(TSNE/UMAP/PCA) and visualization could be also an auxiliary tool for this process.

Technical proposal: Use Sentiment Analysis from Spark NLP(John Snow LABS) to derive a Time Series Sentiment data. The sentiment data will be compared with price data of Financial Asset Classes. Statistical analysis such as correlation will be used to rank the asset classes relevant to the RU Conflict.

Q2. (External)Any relationship between RU event and Amazon/eBay/etc stock price?

Business goal: We try to find the relationship between reactions of most people to the RU event and stock price of a certain brand.


Technical proposal: We collect the statistics of sentiments for a specific group of submissions/comments. We do feature extraction for the stock price's time pattern. Finally we measure the relavance between the two.
