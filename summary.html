Conclusion


While the sub-reddit dataset contains different types of data, the most important information remains in the text. We place our main focus on the sentiment analysis and topic modeling. While sentiment analysis gives us the daily average sentiment scores, topic modeling helps us extract the most significant topics and generate the daily topic proportions.

Sentiment and topic can be seen as two major components of text data. Sentiment focuses on Redditor's feelings and experience, while topic focuses on the facts and objective information. We build models with the generated sentiment and topic features to predict commodity daily returns from the external dataset.
